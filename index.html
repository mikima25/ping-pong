Rules

Ping-pong will count up to your number from 1 with the following exceptions

  • Numbers divisible by 3 are replaced by "Ping"
  • Numbers divisible by 5 are replaced by "Pong"
  • Numbers divisible by 15 are replaced by "Ping-Pong"
